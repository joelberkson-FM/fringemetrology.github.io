Our History

A journey of precision and innovation.

2018

The Beginning

Fringe Metrology was founded with a simple goal: to revolutionize 3D scanning technology for industrial applications. Our early research focused on fringe projection deflectometry.

2020

Breakthrough Technology

We launched our first flagship product, FringeScan, which set new standards for accuracy and speed in measuring large, complex surfaces without contact.

2022

Global Expansion

Fringe Metrology expanded its operations into European and Asian markets, partnering with major aerospace and automotive manufacturers to integrate our technology into their production lines.

2024

Next Generation Metrology

Introduction of the FringeShot series, enabling rapid capture technology that allows for even faster inspection cycles while maintaining sub-10 micron accuracy.
