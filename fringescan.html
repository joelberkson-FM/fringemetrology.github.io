Measuring Microns

<2 - 10 µm Root-Mean-Square (RMS)

Our unique calibration method allow us to achieve unparalleled absolute accuracy. That's right. Our accuracy specification defines the absolute error from true shape, meaning if you measure a true flat surface, the output data will truly be flat, within our accuracy tolerance. We specify a 10 µm RMS accuracy at our largest 3 meter size, and 2 µm RMS accuracy at our smallest size.

No more all-day measurements

< 5 minutes : 1000x more data

Measuring surfaces with CMMs and laser trackers one point at a time is exhausting, repetitive, and time consuming, sometimes even taking an entire day of work. Our systems achieve the same or better accuracy and produce data with 1000x higher sampling. All in just a few minutes, instead of hours.
